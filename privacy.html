DATA STORAGE AND SECURITY

Our company is committed to protecting the privacy of your personal information. We do not sell your personal information, however, third-party cookies may be placed on our site for advertising and marketing purposes, which may be considered a "sale" under data protection laws. We may share information about you for the purposes of providing our services, for legal and investigative purposes, in connection with sweepstakes and promotions, or in case of a merger or acquisition.

We may share non-identifying information with third parties, but we do not share personal information with them for marketing purposes unless we provide you with the opportunity to opt-in or opt-out. Information about freelancers may be shared with clients, agencies, payroll vendors, and partners of SproutGigs. Information about clients and agencies may be shared with freelancers for transaction purposes, but it must not be used for marketing unless the client has expressly consented.

We may use third-party companies and individuals to facilitate our services, but they will only have access to your personal information to perform tasks on our behalf. If you agree to receive information from third parties, your personal information will be subject to their privacy policy. We may share information with government agencies as required by law or for enforcement purposes, and will disclose information about you if we believe it necessary to protect our property, rights, or safety.

YOUR RIGHTS

As an individual, you have certain rights and choices when it comes to your personal information. If you are located in the European Economic Area (EEA), the United Kingdom (UK), or Switzerland, you have additional rights under applicable data protection laws. You can exercise these rights by contacting us or submitting a request on our site.

Here's a summary of your rights:

  • Access: You have the right to request access to your personal information that we hold.
  • Correction: If your personal information is inaccurate or outdated, you have the right to ask us to correct it.
  • Deletion: You have the right to request the deletion of your personal information in certain circumstances.
  • Restriction: You have the right to request that we restrict the processing of your personal information under certain circumstances.
  • Objection: You have the right to object to the processing of your personal information, including for marketing purposes.
  • Data Portability: You have the right to receive a copy of your personal information in a structured, commonly used and machine-readable format.
  • Withdrawal of Consent: If you have previously consented to the processing of your personal information, you have the right to withdraw your consent at any time.
  • Automated Processing: You have the right to request that we restrict any automated processing of your personal information.
  • Complaint: You have the right to file a complaint with the appropriate supervisory authority if you believe that your rights under applicable data protection laws have been violated.

California Residents' Privacy Rights: As a resident of the State of California, you have certain rights regarding the collection and storage of your personal information, as outlined in the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA) and California Privacy Rights Act (CPRA).

  • Right to Know: You have the right to request information about our collection and use of your personal information in the past twelve (12) months. This includes information on the categories of personal information collected, sources of the information, specific pieces of personal information, business or commercial purpose for collecting or selling the information, and categories of third-party recipients to whom the information was sold or shared.
  • Right to Opt-Out of Sale or Sharing: You have the right to opt-out of the sale of your personal information by submitting a request on our website or by contacting us through the information provided in our "Contact Us" section. We do not sell the personal information of individuals under 18 years of age.
  • Right to Limit Use of Sensitive Information: You have the right to request that we limit our use of sensitive personal information to only what is necessary for the provision of our services or for specific business purposes outlined in the CCPA and CPRA.
  • Right to Delete: You have the right to request the deletion of your personal information that we have collected and retained, subject to certain exceptions. This can be done by submitting a request on our website or by contacting us through the information provided in our "Contact Us" section. We may deny the request if we determine that retaining the information is necessary under one or more exceptions listed in the CCPA.
  • Right to Correct: You have the right to request correction of any inaccurate personal information we maintain about you, and we will use commercially reasonable efforts to do so.
  • Non-Discrimination: We will not discriminate against you for exercising your privacy rights as outlined in the CCPA.
  • Verifying Your Request: Only you or a person authorized to act on your behalf may make a request related to your personal information. The request must be verifiable, and we may require you to provide sufficient information to verify your identity or authority. A reasonable fee may be charged or we may refuse to act on the request if it is excessive, repetitive, or manifestly unfounded.

If you would like to exercise any of these rights, please reach out to us through the contact information provided in our "Contact Us" section.
