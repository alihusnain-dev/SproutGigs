Frequently
Asked Questions

Freelancers must maintain a success rate of above 75% at all times (Satisfied tasks). Too many Not-Satisfied tasks will lower Freelancer's success rate. Low success rate prevents a Freelancer from performing jobs for the next 30 days.

Businesses pay only for tasks they are satisfied with. SproutGigs does not charge a fees for tasks that do not meet requirements set in the job. All job owners will have a chance to rate every submitted task.

WE DO NOT APPROVE JOBS ASKING FREELANCERS TO:

  • Complete too many tasks in a single job
  • Click ads/pop ups, unlock a page, refresh page X times, reload, Play games (or similar) in order to finish the required task
  • Spam or harm another website or person
  • Promote Violent/Violence-related content or programs
  • Promote illegal activities
  • Involve websites dedicated to pornography
  • Exchange money between payment systems
  • Disclose personal information (unless you select Leads)
  • Use a credit card or Provide Bank Account Details, or use any form of payment
  • Sign-up for sites that require Membership or Activation Fees
  • Sign-up for a free 14-day trial or similar offers that might risk involuntary payment after free offer expires
  • Transfer of Funds
  • Any jobs involving Coinbase, PayPal, AWSurveys, Amazon, MTURK , Yelp, Craigslist, Google Adsense, Piratebay, Google Voice, Alibaba, Google reviews, Uphold, Upstox, Crewdle, Grammarly, Purchase of old accounts.
  • Create fake reviews or testimonials of products or services.

In order to maintain a world class 24/7 platform with a fully staffed administration team, we must charge fees. Please think of our administrators as your work team partners. We charge fees to approve every job, correct jobs, monitor the submmitted tasks, and provide support services. Job approval fee is $0.65. Fee for tasks rated Satisfied is 7% charged to both freelancer and buyer.
